UniAI

UniAI, a smart chatbot designed to simplify and enhance the student experience at Woxsen University. Powered by cutting-edge machine learning models like LLAMA 3.2, UniAI is here to assist students in navigating through the college admission process, provide instant answers to campus-related queries, and ensure a smooth journey for both prospective and current students.



Why UniAI?



UniAI is more than just a chatbot. It’s your personal guide to everything Woxsen. Whether you have questions about courses, campus life, or need help with admission procedures, UniAI offers quick, reliable, and accurate information. It's designed to make your experience seamless and stress-free.



Our Mission



Our Mission At UniAI, we aim to integrate artificial intelligence into education by offering students a platform that adapts to their needs, responds intelligently, and grows with every interaction. We are constantly evolving, learning from users, and refining the experience to provide the best support for the Woxsen community.



How it Works?



UniAI is trained on a robust dataset using advanced AI models to understand and respond to your questions with precision. Our chatbot is built to handle queries in real-time, enabling quick responses with accurate data about the university. It’s accessible 24/7 and is continually learning to provide better answers over time.
