UniAI's Model 1.0

We are introducing the first AI chatbot for Universities.

UniAI Model 1.0 is the first version of our AI chatbot, trained using the LLAMA 3.2 model to assist students with their college admission queries. It efficiently handles both text and voice inputs, offering quick and accurate responses. Powered by advanced natural language processing, UniAI Model 1 is designed to understand diverse queries and provide relevant answers, making student interactions smoother and more intuitive. This foundational model sets the stage for continuous improvements in AI-driven student support.
