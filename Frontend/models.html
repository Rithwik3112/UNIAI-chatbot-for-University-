Model 1.0

Overview

UniAI Model 1.0 is the first version of our AI chatbot, trained using the LLAMA 3.2 model to assist students with their college admission queries. It efficiently handles both text and voice inputs, offering quick and accurate responses. Powered by advanced natural language processing, UniAI Model 1 is designed to understand diverse queries and provide relevant answers, making student interactions smoother and more intuitive. This foundational model sets the stage for continuous improvements in AI-driven student support.

Information Sources

The data and insights used in our model are derived from a variety of reputable and trusted sources, including:

Academic Journals and Research Papers

Our model has been trained using cutting-edge research published in fields like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and natural language processing. These sources help ensure that the model stays updated with the latest advancements in technology and innovation.

Public Datasets

We utilize a wide range of publicly available datasets, which include diverse and comprehensive information from areas like linguistics, healthcare, and education. These datasets allow our model to better understand and respond to real-world queries effectively.

University Databases

Leveraging data from well-established university repositories, our model incorporates a vast range of knowledge covering everything from student admissions to administrative processes.

User Input and Feedback

Continuous learning from user interactions and feedback has allowed the model to adapt and improve its ability to provide helpful responses over time. This data is anonymized and used in compliance with ethical data usage practices.
